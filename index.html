What is the Gron Digital Platform?

The Gambling and Betting Platform where transactions are administered by cryptographically verifiable code with no human intervention. Smart contracts act as the autonomous trust agent. Grond Digital is the secure, transparent, auditable and fair gaming platform on the Blockchain. The Platform is run with GRO, a utility token used by participants in exchange for value and services they provide. The service providers to the industry, bookmakers, casino operators, affiliates, regulators, and players are just some examples of the Gron Digital eco-system.
