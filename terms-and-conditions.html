  • Pool for team incentives and bonuses: 114 000 000 (One Hundred and Fourteen million) GRO

3.5

3.6

Company may also request certain optional information, such as an email address, through the web interface.

The web application will then send you a unique deposit address for Ether. The Company anticipates that delivery of the Tokens from the Smart Contract System to purchasers will occur within one hour, but reserves the right to delay delivery up to 24 hours.
