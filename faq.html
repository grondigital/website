What is Gron Digital?

Gron Digital is a secure, transparent, auditable and fair open Gaming Platform on the Blockchain. The Platform is operated by a utility token – called GRO.

What cryptocurrency can be used to purchase GRO Utility Tokens?

We accept ETH, BTC and BCH. However, for the BTC and BCH contributions you can make a special enquiry during the pre-ICO round.

What is Token compatible Ethereum wallet?

This is an Ethereum wallet that you can use to store your tokens and participate in our token sale. The following wallets are known to be compatible with Ethereum tokens (ERC-20 standard):

  • MyEtherWallet (no download needed)
  • MetaMask (Firefox and Chrome browser add-on)
  • Mist (Desktop)
  • Parity (Desktop)
  • imToken (iPhone)
  • imToken (Android)
  • Trust (iPhone)
  • Trust (Android)
  • Cipher (iPhone)
  • Cipher (Android)

What do I need to participate in your token sale and buy some GRO?

To participate in our token sale you need a Token compatible Ethereum wallet (web-based or an app, see the list above) where you and you only hold the private keys. Private keys are needed to correctly interact with smart contract functions, like transferring tokens.

What are the Incompatible wallets?

The incompatible wallets are the ones that do not support the ERC20 tokens generated by ICO campaigns. Do not use the following wallets to participate in our token sale. First, move your ETH to a compatible wallet (see the list above):

  • Any Bitcoin exchange
  • Any Ethereum exchange
  • Jaxx
  • Exodus
  • Coinbase
  • Poloniex
  • Kraken
  • Bitstamp
  • Bitfinex
  • Bitrexx
  • Cex.io

What if I have already sent my contribution from the Incompatible wallet?

Don't worry, your contribution is safe. You need to do the following:

  1. Register the wallet from the list of Token compatible Ethereum wallets (see the list above);
  2. Generate the new receiving address to receive your GRO;
  3. Send us the new address;
  4. Lastly, send us the ETH address and the ETH amount where you have made the contribution from.

With our next manual token issuance run, we will send your GRO to your new address.

Why do you insist I contribute from my Ethereum wallet and not an exchange?

Gron Digital issues GRO and sends them to the Ethereum address from where the contribution was made. If you send us the Ethereum from an exchange, we will send GRO back to that exchange wallet, which is not exactly yours, it is shared by many users and controlled by the exchange itself. There will be no way to recover your GRO.

How many GRO can I purchase?

We have only allocated 100m GRO for the Pre-ICO round. You may purchase an entire unsold balance on a first-come-first-served basis.

How do I see my GRO in my ERC20 compatible wallet after I make my contribution?

Once you have made your contribution, the GRO smart contracts will issue your contributing Ethereum address with your GRO automatically. You will need to add a custom token to your wallet to see your GRO tokens. Different wallets have different procedures, the basic information needed to add the custom token in all cases is shown below:

Token Address: 0x997e3ADb550A85895F5BECF54a2751E6dF24edC8
Token Symbol: GRO
Decimals: 18

When will I receive my GRO after I've made my contribution?

As soon as your funds have been received and verified you can expect your GRO to be issued within a few minutes and sent back to your contributing Ethereum address.

What exchanges will GRO be traded at and when?

We are currently in the process of listing GRO with major exchanges. As soon as the process is complete we will communicate to our customers. Our target to list GRO at the end of the ICO by no later than 5 April.

What is the expected value of GRO at the time of listing?

While the ICO is running GRO token is coupled with ETH price, i.e. 1 GRO = 0,0001 ETH. The minimum listed price will be 1 GRO = 0.0001 ETH

What is 1-in-10 Bonus?

In addition to our +40% Pre-ICO Bonus, we are also running the 1-in-10 Lottery, whereby you, the contributor, will get an additional 10% of GRO, if your contribution transaction is written in the block number ending with “0”. In other words, the block number is a multiple of 10. You can check if you are a winner on the Etherscan website. This is an automated process if you are a winner the Gron Digital smart contract will reward you with an additional 10% of GRO.

Can I earn by referring my friends or my audience to participate in Gron Digital ICO?

Yes. We run an affiliate programme. Visit "Become an affiliate" section for detailed instructions.

How much will I earn and get paid?

We pay 20% of every successful GRO transaction and recurring transactions from your affiliate link.

How will I get paid?

Gron Digital’s affiliate programme pays in GRO Tokens to your ETH address.

When will I get paid?

Payments are processed monthly and you will receive your GRO Token earnings the month after the referral purchase was made, with a minimum payment threshold of $20.
